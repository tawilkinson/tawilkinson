The Site

The name of Tinker, Tailor, Soldier, Sponge sums up my intention for this blog fairly neatly – these will be the 4 major categories of posts:

  • Tinker – a catch-all term for DIY and computer related geekery
  • Tailor – costume design/construction for fancy dress purposes (or even porpoises)
  • Soldier – Airsoft related activities
  • Sponge – cookery (mostly cakes)

Also I used to have two blogs and have now added sections from there:

  • PhD - random mutterings about my research project
  • Reviews - reviews of all sorts of geekery
  • Tea - the origin of the other blog, tea reviews aplenty on a bi-weekly basis
