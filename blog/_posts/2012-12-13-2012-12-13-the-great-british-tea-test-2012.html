The 'Paper'

I decided to present this data in the style of a scientific paper. Obviously I decided to use LaTeX to typeset this. Handily the American Physical Society provide Revlatex TeX 4.1 which provide macros to generate papers in the style of different APS journals. I chose to produce my 'paper' in the style of Phys. Rev. A.

Due to only having Excel available I had some poor looking graphs. This was, therefore, a chance for me to try using LaTeX to make graphs using pgfplots and TikZ. This didn't take particularly long and just required following the examples in the manual with a little bit of help from people at Stack Overflow.
